Why ICT fits me

I chose ICT because I enjoy solving logical problems and turning ideas into something practical. Even though I sometimes get distracted, I like the challenge of keeping my brain active and learning how technology works. This study feels like the right environment to grow those problem-solving skills.

This year I want to master the basics of HTML and CSS, get comfortable with GitHub, and take my first steps in JavaScript. I plan to practice by building small projects, committing them weekly, and asking for feedback. At the same time, I'm working on better time management so I stay consistent throughout the programme.
