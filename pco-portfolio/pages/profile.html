Profile

My name is Mikołaj Makulski, I come from Poland and I'm a first-year HBO-ICT student. I've always been curious about how technology works and how new developments can change the world. After a break since high school, I'm excited to challenge myself again, return to coding, and eventually combine my passion for technology with practical skills.

In my free time I enjoy gaming, travelling and spending time with friends outdoors. I love meeting new people and exploring different cultures. Snowboarding, skateboarding, and going to the gym help me stay active, while cooking and music give me ways to relax.

These experiences shape the kind of person I am in my studies as well. Travelling and meeting new people taught me to stay open-minded and curious, while sports and gaming trained my focus and persistence. I try to bring these qualities with me into ICT: being adaptable, willing to learn, and motivated to find solutions even when challenges take time. Below I've listed some of the traits that I think describe me best.

Traits

  • Loyal and supportive
  • Open-minded and curious
  • Critical thinker and problem solver
  • Adaptable in solo or team settings
  • Loves new challenges
