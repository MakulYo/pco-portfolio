Profile

My name is Mikołaj Makulski, I come from Poland and I'm a first-year HBO-ICT student. I've always been curious about how technology works and how new developments like AI, blockchain, or quantum processors can change the world. After a break since high school, I'm excited to challenge myself again, return to coding, and eventually combine my passion for technology with practical skills.

In my free time I enjoy gaming, travelling (I've visited more than 30 countries), and spending time with friends outdoors. I love meeting new people and exploring different cultures. Snowboarding, skateboarding, and going to the gym help me stay active, while cooking and music give me ways to relax. I'm open to working both independently and in teams: I like helping others, but I also value the deep focus I can reach when working solo. My friends would probably describe me as loyal, funny, and smart, while I see myself as an open-minded problem solver who enjoys challenges.

Traits

  • Loyal and supportive
  • Open-minded and curious
  • Critical thinker and problem solver
  • Adaptable in solo or team settings
  • Loves new challenges
