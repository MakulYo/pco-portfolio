FAQ

▶ How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  1. Connect to the HZ network (campus Wi-Fi or wired).
  2. Print → select the HZ “FollowMe” queue.
  3. Go to any HZ printer, tap your student card.
  4. Select your job and press Print.
▶ How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. At the printer, tap your card → choose Scan.
  2. Place the document → choose your HZ email as destination.
  3. Press Start; download the PDF from your email.
▶ How can I buy something on the HZ webshop?
  1. Open the HZ webshop and sign in.
  2. Find the product/event and add to cart.
  3. Checkout and keep the confirmation email.

Open HZ site

▶ How can you book a project space?
  1. Open the room booking tool (via MyHZ/Facilities) and sign in.
  2. Select building, date, and time; filter for project rooms.
  3. Pick an available slot and confirm.
▶ What are the instructions for parking at the HZ parking lot?
  1. Check the campus parking location and opening times.
  2. Bring your student card / follow visitor instructions at the gate.
  3. Park in designated areas and follow on-site signs.
