FAQ

▶ How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  1. Connect to the HZ network (campus Wi-Fi or wired).
  2. Open your document and choose Print → select the HZ printer queue (e.g., FollowMe).
  3. Send the job, then go to any HZ printer.
  4. Tap your student card, choose your job on screen, and press Print.
▶ How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. At the printer, tap your student card and choose Scan.
  2. Place the document and choose the destination (your HZ email).
  3. Press Start; the PDF is mailed to you—download it on your laptop.
▶ How can I buy something on the HZ webshop?
  1. Open the HZ webshop and sign in with your HZ account.
  2. Find the product/event (e.g., intro activity), add to cart.
  3. Check out and keep the receipt/confirmation in your email.

Open HZ site

▶ How can you book a project space?
  1. Go to the room booking tool (via MyHZ/Facilities) and sign in.
  2. Select building, date, and time; filter for project rooms.
  3. Pick an available slot and confirm the reservation.
▶ What are the instructions for parking at the HZ parking lot?
  1. Check your campus parking location and opening times.
  2. Bring your student card or follow visitor instructions at the gate.
  3. Park in the designated student/visitor areas and follow on-site signs.
