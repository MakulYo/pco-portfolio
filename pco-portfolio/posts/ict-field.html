ICT Field

Posted by Mikołaj • 2025-09-11

I follow new tech like AI, blockchain and quantum processors, so I’m drawn to companies that build tools in those spaces. I’m also interested in studios and platforms connected to gaming.

For each company I look at what they build, the kind of roles they hire for, and whether their culture values learning. The goal is to map where my skills could fit after I build a solid base this year.

As I learn more, I’ll add three concrete companies here with links and a short note on why each one fits me.
