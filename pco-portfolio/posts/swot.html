SWOT

Posted by Mikołaj • 2025-09-11

I compared different programmes and kept coming back to HBO-ICT because it mixes logical problem-solving with making real products. I like the idea of turning ideas into something practical that people can use.

Talking to students/teachers and reading the course materials made it clear that the first year focuses on fundamentals like HTML/CSS, Git/GitHub, and basic programming. That matches how I want to re-enter coding after a long break.

My goal is to build small projects consistently, ask for feedback, and improve week by week. That steady rhythm is what will make this choice pay off for me.
