Personal SWOT

Posted by Mikołaj • 2025-09-11

Strengths: curious, open-minded, good at problem solving; willing to help others and ask for help.

Weaknesses: focus can drift; I'm rebuilding study habits after time away from school.

Opportunities: beginner-friendly web projects, feedback from classmates/teachers, and new tech that motivates me to learn.

Threats: time pressure and distractions if I don't plan well.

Action points: keep a weekly plan, ship small pieces often, and reflect after feedback. That turns the SWOT into a study routine.
